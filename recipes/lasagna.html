lasagna

description

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week.

Just bake for an extra 45 mins

ingredients

  • 2 olive oil
  • 750g lean mince beef
  • 90g proscuitto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125 ball mozzarella, torn into strips

steps

  1. brown the meat in a pan with the olive oil

  2. chop the prosciutto and stir some into the mix

  3. pour over the passata, add the beef stock, add the nutmeg

  4. simmer until sauce thickens

  5. heat the oven and lightly oil an ovenproof dish

  6. spoon a third of the meat sauce into the dish and cover with the lasagna sheets and sperad a liitle white sauce over the top

  7. repeat until there are three layers, make sure there no pasta sticking through

  8. scatter the mozzarella over the top

  9. arrange the rest of the proscuitto over the top and bake for about 45 mins, until the top bubbling and slightly browned

pizza

description

classic italian dish, a dough base with a cheese and tomato layer, can be topped with almost any ingredient

great fun for kids to get involved with

ingredients

for the base

  • 300g strong flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzle

for the tomato sauce

  • 100ml passata
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 garlic clove (crushed)

for the topping

  • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced or grated
  • plus anything else e.g. pepperoni, peppers, chicken

steps

  1. make the base: put the flour in a bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt. make a well in the middle, pour in 200ml warm water, the olive oil, and bring together to form a fairly wet dough. tuen onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 mins

  2. make the sauce: mix the passata, basil, and crushed garlic together, season to taste and leave to stand at room temperature

  3. roll out the dough: split the dough into 2 and for each, place onto a floured surface and using a rolling pin proceed to roll into flat bases roughly 25cm in diameter

  4. top and bake: heat the oven to 240C. place the base onto a baking tray and spoon sauce onto the base, spread it out and scatter the cheese on, next add whatever toppings have been selected. place into the oven and cook for 8 - 10 mins until the edges go golden
